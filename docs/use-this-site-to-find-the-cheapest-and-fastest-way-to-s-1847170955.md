> *   Start by entering your origin zip code or city name in the "Departure City" field. Select from the autocomplete options.
> *   Do the same for the "Arrival City" field. This field is used for the destination city of the package.
> *   After filling in the city, enter the length, height, width and weight of your package. You can change the default unit through the drop-down menu.
> *   Click the [Get Rate] button.
> *   The button will only be activated after you have filled in all fields. And it takes about 10 seconds or more to calculate and retrieve the freight.

# 知道你的 COVID 抵押延期权

> 原文：<https://lifehacker.com/know-your-covid-mortgage-forbearance-rights-1846192466>

新 CFPB 报道 称，一些抵押贷款人在 COVID 期间未能遵守抵押延期法。贷款人在权利方面误导借款人，强加未经授权的处罚，和 [错误地驱逐房客](https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2020/11/18/despite-federal-law-protecting-them-hundreds-renters-may-have-been-wrongfully-evicted-during-pandemi/6338228002/) 。以下是你需要知道的，以及如何举报无良贷款人。

### **了解你作为借款人的权利**

《关爱法案》呼吁，如果你声称因 COVID 而陷入财务困境，在 2021 年 3 月 31 日之前完全冻结支付。选择进入的联邦支持贷款的借款人将能够从请求之日起暂停付款，包括利息或罚款，最长 360 天(这不适用于私人贷款，但许多银行自愿提供 180 天的付款减免)。

此外，该法律 **—** 与总统拜登 **—** 最近发布的行政命令一起规定，止赎和与止赎相关的驱逐行动必须暂停，直到 2021 年 3 月 31 日。CARES 法案还包括如果给予救济，则拒绝负面信用报告的条款。

### **如何申请隐忍**

这一部分很简单:你只需要问你的贷款人(如果你通过电话要求宽容，获得书面记录或至少做笔记可能是个好主意)。传闻证据表明，T2 的贷款人通常也不会要求提供困难证明。

### **不要被你的贷款人误导**

现在你知道你的权利，确保你的贷款人没有藐视规则。根据，这方面的例子包括:

> *   Drag out the handling of forbearance request. CFPB said that in some cases, service personnel are slow to handle extension requests. As a result, some borrowers can't repay their loans and their credit scores are hit.
> *   Tolerate the borrower without the borrower's knowledge. In other cases, they think they just read about patience on the website of service providers, or discuss financial issues with representatives on the phone. Those borrowers don't understand that they have applied for deferred payment, or that the service organization will handle deferred payment.
> *   Ranger collection notice. CARES Act promises that borrowers don't have to worry about mortgage payment within six months to one year. However, some service providers have issued notices informing borrowers that their accounts are overdue, and they may face late payment fees and lower credit scores. According to CFPB, these notices "may cause confusion to consumers who join the Care Act".
> *   Misleading statement about one-time payment. CARES Act does not require the borrower to pay a large sum of money for the missed repayment after the deferred repayment ends. Instead, the borrower resumes monthly payments. However, CFPB said that some service personnel told borrowers that they needed a one-time payment to make up for the missed monthly payment after all deferred repayments were due.

### **如何投诉**

首先直接联系你的贷款人或贷款服务机构，因为他们能够最快地解决你的问题(特别是如果是相对较小的事情，可能只是一个错误，比如处理时间太长的延期请求)。

如果这不起作用，或者你认为你的贷款人故意绕过《关爱法案》制定的法律，请向消费者金融保护局(CFPB)投诉 [这里](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/complaint/) 。CFPB 将代表您寻求解决方案(大多数公司将在 15 天内对投诉做出回应)。
